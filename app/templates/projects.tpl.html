Wat Wars

Objective: Wat Wars is a turn-based game built in JavaScript. Users are able to select a character and an opponent, then go to battle by selecting one of four moves from the battle screen. Each attack does random amounts of damage and the outcome of each fight is a different result. (Who wouldn't want to pit Bob Ross vs. Barney Stinson?!)
